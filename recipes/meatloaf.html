Meatloaf

Description

A meatloaf is a mixture of ground beef and other ingredients that has been shaped into a loaf, then baked. When you're in need of delicious comfort food that everyone in your household will love, a traditional meatloaf is the way to go. It's a classic meal, and it's an incredibly simple one too.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chuck
  • Vegetables
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Glaze

Steps

  1. Chop the Vegetables
  2. Form the Meatloaf
  3. Bake
  4. Make the Glaze
  5. Glaze the Meatloaf
